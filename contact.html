For the Profession

For consulting engineers, architects, and HVAC specialists.

We supply CAD files, BIM models, and integration drawings to specifying engineers working on cigar lounge fit-outs, hospitality projects, and HVAC retrofits. Voltage and frequency options (110V/220V, 50/60Hz), BMS interface specifications for the in-duct series, and certification documentation (CE, FCC, RoHS) are available upon request.

Address technical correspondence to engineering@praanandco.com, citing the project name and a brief description. The matter shall be routed to the appropriate engineer.
